Manado

For those who like snorkeling or diving activities, the name Manado is certainly familiar. The capital city of North Sulawesi Province is indeed famous for its marine tourism, namely Bunaken Island, where visitors can see the beauty of various marine life. In this city, the majority of the population still preserves European - style building architecture with wooden windows and high ceilings. Not surprisingly, the traditional atmosphere is still strong here.

If you visit Manado, don't just visit Bunaken Island. Tangkoko - Batuangas Dua Saudara National Park – home to protected animals – is also a worthy tourist destination. In this place, tourists can see tarsiers, maleo birds, hornbills, anoa and babirusa. After being satisfied traveling around, a vacation in Manado is not complete without tasting various types of typical delicious dishes, such as Cakalang Fufu, Roa Fish, Red Bean Soup (Brenebon), Panada, and Red Bean Ice.
